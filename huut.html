A Voice based Social Media Platform

Huut is a audio social media platform to explore, showcase talent and to create valuable contents with the power of voice.

Entertainment PlatformProduct Design

Problem Statement

Traditional social media platforms predominantly rely on written texts, images, and short videos, leading to a limited range of expression for users. Many individuals struggle with low self-esteem and self-consciousness related to their personal appearance. Effective writing is a critical aspect of communication, especially in the digital world.

Solution

  • Huut offers innovative and user-friendly features, including advanced audio editing tools, real-time collaborative options, discoverability algorithms tailored for vocal talents, and support for diverse languages and accents.
  • By providing a voice-centric social media space, talented individuals from various backgrounds can finally connect with their audience, gain recognition, and thrive in a community that truly appreciates the beauty of vocal expression.

User Interview

Qualitative Research

We interviewed 15-20 people, inorder to retrive the indepth insights on their daily activity with social network applications. We noted that what aspects they consider for solving their problems

  • What makes you to move away from video and text on social media?
  • How comfortable you are to share your thoughts on social media?
  • What keeps you engaged with existing social media apps?
  • How much time you spend regularly on social media apps?

Quantitative Research

We did a survey on usage of social media application to users and recieved an ample of responses on their need, usage and the form of media they preferred to use.

Which social media app you use the most?

  • Facebook - 28%
  • Instagram - 42%
  • Twitter - 18%
  • Others - 12%

What type of content you prefer the most?

  • Entertainment - 54%
  • Sports - 22%
  • Business - 12%
  • Others - 10%

Which form of media you consume the most?

  • Video - 35%
  • Images - 25%
  • Audio - 20%
  • Text - 20%

What you expect from a social media platform?

  • Valuable contents - 40%
  • Make Conversation - 12%
  • Showcase talent - 28%
  • Make revenue - 20%

User Interview & Survey Outcome

As a feedback from users, we recieved a good amount of valuable insights from customers, Listed below.

  • Spending more time on video content than other formats
  • Need of a platform to showcase talent
  • Hear information rather than watching
  • Need of platform to earn and get popularity
