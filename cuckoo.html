Use the power of cloud service to call or text across the globe.

A service for businesses to make calls, send texts, and have audio/video meetings across different devices using the cloud.

Telecommunication PlatformMobile UX

Problem Statement

Businesses face the challenge of efficiently handling their communication tasks—such as calls, texts, and audio/video meetings—across a variety of devices. The goal is to find effective solutions that leverage cloud technology for streamlined and accessible communication.

Solution

Cuckoo a cloud platform, helps businesses by combining calls, texts, and audio/video meetings in one place. This makes communication smooth across various devices, boosting efficiency, collaboration, and simplifying channel management.

Research

Survey

We did a survey on usage of other calling apps, inorder to retrive the indepth insights on their daily activities. We noted that what aspects they consider for solving their problems

Age Range:
  • 18 - 45
Occupation:
  • Students
  • Professionals
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Others
Sample Questions:
  • How frequently do you make international calls for work or personal reasons?
  • Are there specific regions or countries you frequently call?
  • How easy is it for you to navigate through international calling apps?
  • What features would you appreciate to optimize data usage during calls?

Hypotheses

As a feedback from users, we recieved a good amount of valuable insights from customers, Listed below.

  • Users emphasized the critical importance of high call quality for international calls and expressed a desire for more affordable international calling options.
  • They concerned about data usage during international calls and would appreciate features to optimize data usage during calls.
  • Emphasized the importance of seamless integration with contacts and concerned about the importance of multi-platform accessibility.

Conclusion

The survey indicates a strong demand for a Cloud-Based International Calling App that prioritizes high call quality, affordability, user-friendly interfaces, and robust features such as integration with contacts and messaging capabilities.
