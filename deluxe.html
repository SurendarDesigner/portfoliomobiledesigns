South Indian based OTT Platform

Deluxe is a an multilingual OTT platform works on mobile, web and TV. Providing rich handfree user experience for the people.

Entertainment PlatformProduct Design

Problem Statement

Rural people in South India are more interested to watch their regional movies through a single online platform. They love to watch other regional blockbuster movies on their native language. Looking for a easy to use handfree experience on finding movies and playing without hassle.

Solution

  • As a part of our finding, an multilingual OTT platform that.
  • Supports all major languages of south region of India.

User Interview

Qualitative Research

We interviewed 15-20 people, inorder to retrive the indepth insights on their daily activity with social network applications. We noted that what aspects they consider for solving their problems

  • How frequently you use OTT platforms?
  • How comfortable are you to use OTT on mobile platform?
  • What keeps you engaged with existing OTT platforms?
  • How much time you spend regularly on OTT platforms?
  • Supports all major languages of south region of India.

Quantitative Research

We did a survey on usage of social media application to users and recieved an ample of responses on their need, usage and the form of media they preferred to use.

What is your preferreed language on OTT platform?

  • Tamil - 42%
  • Telugu - 28%
  • Kannada - 18%
  • Malayalam - 12%

What is your preferred platform to watch OTT?

  • Mobile - 52%
  • Desktop - 12%
  • TV - 30%
  • All of the above - 6%

Would you prefer to watch dubbed movies?

  • Yes - 56%
  • No - 44%

What is your preferred action type to search movies?

  • Typing text - 22%
  • Voice commands - 78%

User Interview & Survey Outcome

As a feedback from users, we recieved a good amount of valuable insights from customers, Listed below.

  • People preferr to have OTT platform on mobile
  • They prefer hand free experience to control the application
  • People from tamil nadu preferred to use OTT platform more
  • People prefer to watch dubbed movies on OTT platform
