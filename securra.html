Problem Statement

In today's fast-paced and sedentary lifestyle, individuals face significant challenges in maintaining optimal health and achieving desired fitness levels. A growing number of people are recognising the importance of proactive health management and are seeking structured healthcare programs to monitor their well-being and enhance their fitness.

Solution

Securra is a modern health program that uses advanced health tech, personalised coaching, behaviour strategies, and data insights to help people manage their health, reach fitness goals, and stay well."

Research

Survey

Convenience sampling of 500 individuals aged 18-45 who enrol in the Securra health program. The survey will include questions related to health behaviours, fitness goals, coaching experiences, behaviour change strategies, and the utilisation of data insights.

Descriptive statistics will be used to summarize participant demographics and baseline characteristics. Paired-sample t-tests and ANOVA will be used to compare pre-test and post-test scores related to health behaviors, adherence to fitness goals, and utilization of program components.

Hypotheses

As a feedback from users, we recieved a good amount of valuable insights from customers, Listed below.

  • Participants' health management behaviors will improve significantly after three months of participating in the Securra health program.
  • The personalized coaching and behavior change strategies within the program will be associated with increased adherence to fitness goals.
  • Participants who actively utilize data-driven insights provided by the program will demonstrate better health outcomes compared to those who do not.

Conclusion

Through this research, we aim to shed light on the impact of the Securra health program in promoting improved health management and fitness outcomes among participants. The findings will serve as a basis for refining the program and developing evidence-based strategies for effective health interventions.
